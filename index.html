About Me (in which, I shamelessly promote myself):

I'm currently enrolled in the coding bootcamp at the University of Richmond, and I am a sales associate at Sneed's Nursery.

Click on projects from the menu to see examples of my work with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JQuery, AJAX, Firebase, and Moment.js. More to come....

A Brief History:

I left high school two years early (because who wouldn't?) to attend Mary Baldwin College through the Program for the Exceptionally Gifted. I transferred to Virginia Commonwealth University and graduated with a B.S. in anthropology and sociology.

I joined the Peace Corps and traveled to Niger as a Natural Resources Management volunteer. Turns out I'm allergic to mefloquine. Ay ga ba Niger boobo, amma ay sinda baani. Jam bombata!

I completed a three-year small farms program on The Living Farm in Paonia, CO. I learned to grow veggies, raise sheep, and catch chickens, as well as how to market produce effectively.

I brought sheep and chickens from Paonia home to Virginia and raised them for a couple of years on family land. Milk, meat, wool, and cuteness in abundance.

I went to work at Lowe's, becoming a Department Manager and then a Live Nursery Specialist. The great thing about retail is that it teaches you many skills, and that eventually it encourages you to find your passion... elsewhere.

I decided to move into a new industry, and I chose the full-stack bootcamp at the University of Richmond as the first step. How nice that my natural ability to spot abused or missing apostrophes (or other misunderstood punctuation) may have real value to someone.
